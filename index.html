RAD MUTUAL EDUCATION

Our Mission

Rad Mutual Education is a mutual-aid driven organization making science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM) education accessible to everyone within our community through skillshare, mentoring, and technology access. We believe in structuring educational plans around consensus building between students, instructors and their communities that account for students’ holistic needs such as emotional, financial and environmental support.

Who We Are

Rad Mutual Education is an organization committed to bringing STEM (science, technology, engineering, & mathematics) education to our communities through organized skillshare and in collaboration with mutual aid organizations. Individually, we are computer science students at Western Washington University who wish to see our communities thrive, and believe that providing STEM education for the youth is one of the most effective ways of doing that.

What We Provide

We provide students within and in association with our communities with free STEM education. At this time, we are only offering free regular computer science and programming lessons for students ages ten and up via Zoom. Your student does not need to know computer science, programming, or math. Each unit we teach will introduce a wide range of important computer science topics and skills.

If you do not have computer or internet access, we will help coordinate with local resources, such as schools, libraries, and community members, to provide suitable technologies. We will provide free snacks, books, and paper activity drop-offs to ensure that we are clearing as many obstacles as possible for kids who would like to learn with us.

Why It's Useful

Exposure to various fields is beneficial to personal growth and understanding - no matter what your student hopes to study someday, learning about computer science is expanding their knowledge of what’s out there.

Where Community Matters

We will continue to grow RME to meet the needs of our community, with immediate efforts to eliminate traditional barriers to learning by collaborating with mutual aid groups. We encourage you to connect with us and share thoughts on how to best support the educational needs of our community as this change occurs.
